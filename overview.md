3. **User Input: Talking to Your Program**: Interactive programs, type conversion

6. **Functions: Your Code Toolbox**: Reusable code, parameters, returns

8. **Math Operations & Problem Solving**: Advanced operators, math module, puzzles

**Goal**: Synthesize skills into creative projects

9. **Animation: Bringing Drawings to Life**: Game loops, timing, interactive graphics
10. **Fractals: Math in Nature**: Recursion, self-similarity, natural patterns
11. **Game Design: Math Challenges**: Complete games, scoring, user interaction
12. **Final Project: Math Art Gallery**: Independent project, debugging, presentation
